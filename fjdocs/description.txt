SafeTicket

What is SafeTicket?

SafeTicket is a secure online platform for buying and selling second-hand event tickets in Israel.
It connects two types of people:
1. People who bought a ticket to an event and can't attend anymore.
2. People who want to buy a ticket but the organizer is sold out.

What problem does SafeTicket solve?

There is no safe marketplace for second-hand tickets in Israel.
Today, people buy/sell on Facebook groups with zero protection — paying via Bit/PayBox/wire and hoping for the best.
Scams are common. SafeTicket eliminates this risk through escrow.

How does it work?

- Buyers pay SafeTicket (via licensed PSP), not the seller directly.
- The ticket is delivered immediately to the buyer (email + SMS permanent link).
- The money is held in escrow until 48 hours after the event.
- If the ticket doesn't work → full refund, no questions asked.
- After the event, money is released to the seller (minus 10% commission).

Business model:
- Buyer pays: ticket price + 10% service fee
- Seller pays: 10% commission on successful sale only
- Listing is free for sellers

Anti-scalping:
- Israeli law prohibits reselling tickets above face value.
- On SafeTicket, asking price must be ≤ face value (enforced by admin review).

Key decisions:
- Guest checkout (no account required for buyers)
- Events are added manually by admins (future: AI-based auto-discovery)
- Seller flow: pick event → upload ticket → set price (admin extracts details)
- App-locked tickets (Eventim etc.) excluded at launch
- Digital tickets only (PDF, screenshot with barcode/QR)
- Brand: safeticket.co.il
