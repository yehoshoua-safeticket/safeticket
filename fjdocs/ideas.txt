Marketing & Growth Ideas (Early Brainstorming)

1. React to social media posts by scammed people — raise awareness of the problem and offer SafeTicket as the solution.
2. Get journalists / influencers / pages to talk about the ticket scam problem — create awareness around the problem before pushing the solution.
3. Users can report potential scammers / unauthorized resellers on social media (helps build community trust).
4. For the first ~100 events, send a co-founder to the venue entrance to physically verify ticket validity with customers — builds confidence and generates content for social proof.
5. Communicate on social media about physical presence at events (stories, posts showing real people entering with SafeTicket tickets).
